# 2026-05-02 Session 002

## Goal
Convert backend usage protection from per-feature daily request quotas to a monthly AI credit model that supports a 7-day trial, a free tier, and two low-pressure paid tiers.

## Context
The desired commercial model is: 7-day free trial, local recording stays free, then users can choose a low-pressure ¥8/month tier or a more flexible ¥16/month tier. A unified AI credit pool lets users spend their allowance across review, assistant, transcription, and record processing according to their own usage patterns while keeping backend cost bounded.

## Implementation
- Replaced per-request daily limits with a monthly AI credit pool.
- Added supported tiers:
  - `trial`: 700 credits for the first 7 days.
  - `free`: 300 credits per month.
  - `daily`: 1500 credits per month, intended for the ¥8/month plan.
  - `deep`: 4500 credits per month, intended for the ¥16/month plan.
  - `reviewer`: high internal/review allowance.
- Preserved `USAGE_PRO_USER_IDS` as a legacy alias mapped to `deep`.
- Added `USAGE_DAILY_USER_IDS`, `USAGE_DEEP_USER_IDS`, `USAGE_TRIAL_DAYS`, `USAGE_CREDIT_LIMIT_OVERRIDES`, and `USAGE_CREDIT_COST_OVERRIDES`.
- Added per-feature default credit costs:
  - quick ack: 0
  - clean: 1
  - atomize: 1
  - tag suggestion: 1
  - assistant chat: 3
  - assistant archive: 8
  - review overview/focused: 5
  - review follow-up: 3
  - transcription: 10 credits/minute
  - hidden tag cluster/normalize: 20
- Made trial use a fixed 7-day trial cycle instead of the monthly billing cycle, so a trial cannot reset by crossing into a new calendar month.
- Changed credit exhaustion responses to HTTP 402 with `error: "ai_credit_exhausted"` so clients do not treat it as normal rate limiting.
- Updated admin pages to show monthly credit usage, remaining credits, tier, trial status, and per-event credit cost.
