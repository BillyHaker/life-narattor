# 2026-05-02 Session 003

## Goal
Add a lightweight user-facing settings page without adding another primary tab.

## Context
The app had privacy consent on first launch and internal DevTools, but no ordinary settings surface for users to revisit AI/privacy/voice/support information after onboarding. The right place is a quiet global entry on the Record page header, not the bottom tab bar.

## Files changed
- `Life Narattor/Screens/RecordFeedScreen.swift`
- `Life Narattor/Screens/AppSettingsScreen.swift`
- `Docs/04_Sessions/2026-05-02_session-003.md`
- `Docs/05_Changes/Change-222-app-settings-screen.md`
- `Docs/VERIFICATION_BACKLOG.md`

## Implementation
- Added a small circular settings button to the Record page date header.
- Added `AppSettingsScreen` as a sheet-based settings surface.
- Added grouped settings content for:
  - AI usage and future quota/subscription visibility.
  - Local-first privacy boundary and third-party AI processing disclosure.
  - Voice transcription permission guidance.
  - Privacy policy, technical support, and app version/build information.
- Kept DevTools out of the public settings surface.
