# Change 147 - AI review facts, links, follow-ups

## What changed
- Changed overview and focused AI review prompts to produce short review notes instead of report-like summaries.
- New response target is:
  - `事实：` direct evidence from records
  - `联系：` weaker links or insights that are not obvious from linear diary reading
  - `可继续问：` one or two short follow-up questions
- Renamed the top analysis card in `SearchScreen` from `AI 分析` to `事实与联系`.

## Why
- The previous output looked like a long analytical report and overwhelmed the screen.
- The product goal is to surface facts first, then reveal less obvious links and inspiration, without overclaiming causality.
